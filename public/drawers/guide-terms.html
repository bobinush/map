Guideline for placement term limits.

To preserve the dynamism of The Borderland, there is a suggested 3 year term limit for placement. This guideline is both a positive and a negative.

  1. A Dream or camp who "invests" in a specific location (doing land work, building work, or buying specialised infrastructure), will have an advantage when negotiating placement the next 2 years.
  2. After 3 years of placement in the same location, one should be expected to move, if challenged in the placement process.
