What are the placement rules?

  • The maximum size for one area or a group of adjacent areas is 1250m². If your space requirements are larger than that, make two (or more) polygons. and add a 5 meter separation (fire buffer)
  • Keep your camp within the yellow placement borders and do not block fire roads
  • You must fill in all info fields. Contact info is important! Without it, your area might be removed.
  • Confirm that your sound intentions match the sound agreements of your zone. Read more in the sound guide.

Keep this in mind

  1. Larger structures, kitchen tents, RVs and vans need 4 meters between them and anything else that is flammable. Ordinary camping tents can be placed anywhere inside your area.
